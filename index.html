ABOUT DIT MUN

MUN Society of DIT University is a newly-found club and family in the university. Though recently established, the society has accomplished great milestones in the progress made so far.

We embark on a journey of diplomacy, debate, and global engagement. We simulate international relations, cultivate critical thinking, and foster a community of passionate individuals committed to addressing the world's pressing issues. Our MUN Society serves as a crucible for cultivating critical thinking, effective communication, and a profound understanding of international relations.

FACULTY ADVISOR

Mrs. Maneesha D’souza

“Model United Nations embarks on a journey of diplomacy, conversation, and cooperation. Expressing your views cultivates empathy, creativity, and understanding among future global leaders. Through the exchange of ideas and collaboration, we can create a transformative impact and ignite meaningful progress.”

FOUNDER

Mr. Ankush Jha

“At the DITMUN society, we don’t treat MUN as a mere extracurricular activity, rather, we see it as our duty to make the students of our country aware of the diplomatic structure and challenges faced by people around the world.”

FOUNDER

Mr. Tejas Nautiyal

“Good oratory skills and a solid research base are essential for the youth of this world. Without them, we are doomed to diplomatic disasters. MUNs expose participants to multiple perspectives and test their capabilities by asking them to look for potential solutions that cater to the majority of the world.”

FOUNDER

Mr. Anavil Nautiyal

“Public speaking is no longer just a need, it is a necessity in this era. A good orator can change the world, but to ensure that the orator changes the world for good, diplomacy and exposure to ground realities are a must.”

FOUNDER
